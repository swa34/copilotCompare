Free (via Chat)

  • Can explain Excel concepts
  • Suggest general formulas
  • Answer questions about Excel features

Pro (In-App)

  • Analyzes your actual spreadsheet data
  • Generates formulas based on your columns
  • Creates charts and PivotTables from natural language
  • Highlights, filters, and sorts data on request
  • Forecasting and trend analysis
  • Python-powered advanced analytics
💡 Real-World Example

A sales manager asks: "Show me the top 5 products by revenue last quarter and create a chart." Copilot Pro analyzes the spreadsheet and generates both the filtered view and visualization instantly.

Note: Copilot in Excel requires AutoSave enabled (file saved to OneDrive) and works best with data formatted as Excel tables.

Real-World Scenarios

See how Copilot Pro solves common workplace challenges.

📈

Data Analysis & Reporting

Challenge: A business analyst needs to identify key trends in monthly sales data but isn't an Excel formula expert.

Pro Solution: In Excel, they ask "Show me the top 5 performing products last quarter" or "Generate a pivot table comparing regional sales growth year-over-year." Copilot analyzes the data and generates insights automatically.

📧

Email Management

Challenge: A manager returns from PTO to 200+ emails and needs to quickly prioritize and catch up.

Pro Solution: In Outlook, Copilot summarizes long threads, highlights urgent items, and drafts responses—turning hours of catch-up into minutes.

🎬

Presentation Creation

Challenge: An HR specialist needs to create a training presentation from a 20-page policy document.

Pro Solution: In PowerPoint: "Create a 10-slide presentation summarizing the new remote work policy from this Word document, focusing on key employee responsibilities." Copilot generates the slides with speaker notes.

👥

Meeting Catch-Up

Challenge: A project lead has back-to-back meetings and missed an important team standup.

Pro Solution: In Teams, Copilot provides an instant summary: decisions made, action items assigned, and who's responsible—without watching a 60-minute recording.

📝

Document Drafting

Challenge: A marketing manager needs to create a campaign brief pulling from multiple source documents.

Pro Solution: In Word: "Draft a campaign brief based on the product specs document, last quarter's results presentation, and the strategy email from the CMO." Copilot synthesizes all three sources into one cohesive document.

Requirements & Notes

To use Copilot Pro in M365 apps, you need:

  • ✓ Copilot Pro subscription
  • ✓ Microsoft 365 Personal or Family subscription (for desktop app access)
  • ✓ Files saved to OneDrive (AutoSave enabled for Excel)

🔒 Data Privacy

Microsoft does not use your chat data, conversations, or attachments to train AI models. Your data is not sold to third parties—this applies to both Free and Pro.
